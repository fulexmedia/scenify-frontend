Common questions
Do tokens expire? ▾
No. One-time tokens (Starter and Pro) never expire. Subscription tokens refresh monthly, and unused ones roll over to the next month — we don't take them back.
What exactly is one token? ▾
One token = one video generation. You upload your photos, arrange the order, choose your settings, and hit Generate. That uses one token regardless of length (up to 15 seconds) or format (16:9 or 9:16).
Can I re-export in a different format without using another token? ▾
Yes. Once a video is generated, you can download it in 16:9 or 9:16 (and in 1080p or 4K if your plan includes it) without using additional tokens. You only spend a token when you generate a new AI video.
How do I cancel the subscription? ▾
Cancel anytime from your account settings. You keep access until the end of the billing period, and any rollover tokens you've accumulated stay in your account.
Is there a refund policy? ▾
If you're not happy with your first video, contact us and we'll refund the token. Beyond that, tokens are non-refundable as they represent AI compute time. We offer 2 free tokens on sign-up so you can try before you buy.
