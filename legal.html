2. What data we collect

  • Account data: Email address, hashed password, account creation date
  • Usage data: Token balance, video generation job IDs, generation timestamps

  • Technical data: IP address (for security and abuse prevention), browser type, access times

5. Third-party processors (Data Processing Agreements in place)

  • Supabase Inc. (auth & database) — EU servers, GDPR compliant
  • Stripe Inc. (payment processing) — PCI DSS Level 1 compliant
  • Higgsfield AI (video generation API) — receives photo URLs transiently for generation only
  • Vercel Inc. (hosting) — EU region available, GDPR compliant

6. Data retention

  • Account data: Until account deletion + 30 days
  • Payment records: 10 years (German tax law, § 147 AO)
  • IP logs: 7 days (security purposes)

  • Generated videos: Not stored (delivered directly to user)
