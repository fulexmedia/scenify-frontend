The process

Not a slideshow.
A walk-through.

Scenify doesn't cut between photos. The AI generates real camera movement between every scene.

01

Drop in your listing photos. Scenify reads each room and plans the camera path automatically.

02

Drag scenes into walking order. The AI generates a transition between each adjacent pair — not random rooms.

03

Camera glides from room to room — through doorways, past windows. No cuts. Download in 16:9 or 9:16.
