The process

Not a slideshow.
A walk-through.

Scenify doesn't cut between photos. The AI generates the movement between them — a real cinematic path through your property.

01
Upload your photos

Drop in your listing photos in any order. Scenify reads each room and plans the camera path automatically.

02
Arrange & customize

Drag scenes into your preferred order, exclude frames, pick camera style — smooth dolly, orbit, or handheld — and set duration up to 15 seconds.

03
AI generates the path

Between every photo, the AI invents fluid camera movement — through a doorway, past a window, into the next room. No cuts. No jarring jumps.
